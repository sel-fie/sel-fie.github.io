cratscendo

so cratscendo was a 10ish week long project that i and 7 other team mates took on for a game competition that gamedev mcgill hosted, called GDM studios. we were given two mentors, a theme, some goals, and then we were told to make a game. it was my first time ever doing this (other than the unity roll-a-ball tutorial). i learned so much from it, and it was truly an amazing experience. as far as what i worked on. i did the character art + animations as well as one of the enemies. i did the majority of 3D modeling and map design as well as a good chunk of gameplay programming. i also produced weekly devlogs that were required for the competition which you can find in this playlist!
