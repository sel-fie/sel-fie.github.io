Games:

cratscendo

i am extremely proud of all the work i did and the work my team did on this game.

so cratscendo was a 10ish week long project that i and 7 other team mates took on for a game competition that gamedev mcgill hosted, called GDM studios. we were given two mentors, a theme, some goals, and then we were told to make a game. it was my first time ever doing this (other than the unity roll-a-ball tutorial). i learned so much from it, and it was truly an amazing experience. as far as what i worked on. i did the all character art + animations as well as one of the enemies. i did the majority of 3D modeling and map design as well as a good chunk of gameplay programming. i also produced weekly devlogs that were required for the competition which you can find in this playlist!


gameplay programming wise, since i was on a team with 6 other programmers, there isn't really something that only i worked on. we really all bounced work off each other, and helped to improve and integrate what others had already worked on. i did originally design the health system as well as a few of the rat attacks. i made the collectibles (3D models + programming). I also designed the room mechanics, such as when the doors close and open, including everything adjacent to that. otherwise i mainly helped to improve and add on to pre-existing code to help our project run smoothly!


for this project, i managed to learn 3D modeling, pixel art, animation, and a lot more about the intricacies of game design and programming.
